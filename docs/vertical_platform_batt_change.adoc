== Changing the battery

The {platform} uses a standard, non-rechargeable CR2032 battery. It is easy to replace and only requires a Phillips screwdriver. Here are the steps to do it.

* Lay the unit on a flat surface with the backplate facing up. image:../includes/dia_vertical_batt_chg_step_01.png[Battery change step 1,800,574,pdfwidth=50%,scaledwidth=50%,role=text-left]
* Remove the Phillips screws at the top of the case (2×). image:../includes/dia_vertical_batt_chg_step_02.png[Battery change step 1,800,574,pdfwidth=50%,scaledwidth=50%,role=text-left]
* Lift the top edge of the backplate about 10° or 12mm or ½in. image:../includes/dia_vertical_batt_chg_step_03.png[Battery change step 1,800,574,pdfwidth=50%,scaledwidth=50%,role=text-left]
* Slide the backplate backwards. image:../includes/dia_vertical_batt_chg_step_04.png[Battery change step 1,800,574,pdfwidth=50%,scaledwidth=50%,role=text-left]
* Use a soft, non-conductive material, like a toothpick or a plastic spudger, to push the dead battery out of its compartment. image:../includes/dia_vertical_batt_chg_step_05.png[Battery change step 1,800,574,pdfwidth=50%,scaledwidth=50%,role=text-left]

Follow these steps backwards until the calculator is closed.
